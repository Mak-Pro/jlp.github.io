About

How Joyful Life Programs Works

The Joyful Life Programs platform is an online resource center where we gather all resources together to get you a joyful life

get joyful life

Why do we do it?

Too many people let fear, regret, anger, or other negative emotions rob them of joy and contentment. Joyful Life Programs was created to equip people to discover and live the abundant life Jesus came to bring.

What is it?

Joyful Life Programs provides information, training, coaching and counseling resources, and community support. The platform features both curated and crowd-sourced content to help learners grow in all areas of life: body, spirit, and soul.

Who is it for?

Spiritually-sensitive adults of all ages who would like greater contentment, a sense of purpose, or the joy of the Lord.

How does the platform work?

Learners engage with distinctive content, transformational experiences, and personal support to learn principles and practices that help them become the shining, joyful masterpieces God created them to be. Resources teach users how to apply the wisdom of the scriptures to walk in confidence and hope each day. Visitors browse through resource libraries in seven categories or take our survey to get personalized recommendations on where to start. The platform remembers what you’ve viewed and collects feedback to guide other users to the content best-suited for them.

Benefits

The Joyful Life Programs platform may provide you with a wide range of tools for a joyful life
